Google Business Setup Guide

How Bethel Pawn is optimized for Google Search and Google Maps — and how to keep it that way.

01

Google Business Profile

Bethel Pawn's Google Business Profile is the single most important local SEO asset. It controls what shows up when someone searches "pawn shop Bethel Ohio" or "pawn shop near me." Keep this information accurate and up to date at all times.

Access it at: business.google.com

  • Business name: Bethel Pawn
  • Address: 119 West Plane St, Bethel, OH 45106
  • Phone: (513) 427-4705
  • Hours: Tue–Fri 10–5, Sat 10–2, Sun–Mon Closed
  • Category: Pawn Shop (primary), Firearms Dealer (secondary)
  • Website: bethelpawn.com

Keep Hours Updated

Inaccurate hours are the #1 complaint customers leave negative reviews about. Update your Google hours immediately any time they change, and add holiday hours in advance.

02

Google Reviews — Your Most Valuable Asset

With 4.9 stars, Bethel Pawn already has an excellent review profile. The goal is to maintain it by consistently asking satisfied customers to leave a review.

  • Ask every satisfied customer directly — "Would you mind leaving us a Google review?"
  • Put your Google review link on your receipt or a business card
  • Respond to every review — positive and negative — professionally
  • Never offer incentives for reviews (violates Google policy)

Getting Your Review Link

In Google Business Profile, go to Home → Get more reviews → Copy the link. Share it directly with customers via text or post it on social media.

03

Google Posts — Stay Active

Google Business Profile allows you to post updates, offers, and news that appear directly in your Google listing. Posting regularly signals to Google that your business is active.

  • Post what you are buying this week ("We're paying top dollar for Milwaukee tools this week")
  • Post new inventory highlights
  • Post holiday hours or closures
  • Aim for at least one post per week
04

Photos — Add Regularly

Businesses with more photos get significantly more clicks and calls from Google. Add photos regularly to stay ahead of competitors.

  • Store exterior — front of building, signage
  • Store interior — showcases, displays, overall feel
  • Team photos — LaMont, Teresa, Brayden, Mike, Camryn
  • Inventory highlights — interesting items, firearms display
  • Add at least 2-3 new photos per month
05

Website SEO — Already Built In

This website was built with local SEO as a priority. Every page includes:

  • Title tags with location keywords (Bethel, Ohio, Clermont County)
  • Meta descriptions with key services and call to action
  • Schema markup (structured data) telling Google this is a PawnShop
  • Canonical tags to prevent duplicate content issues
  • ADA-compliant markup for accessibility
  • Six dedicated SEO landing pages targeting local search terms

Most Important Action

Make sure bethelpawn.com is listed as your website in your Google Business Profile. This connects your site's SEO strength to your local listing.

06

Ongoing Maintenance — What to Do Monthly

  • Check that all Google Business info is still accurate
  • Add 2-3 new photos to Google Business Profile
  • Post 4+ Google updates (weekly is ideal)
  • Respond to any new reviews
  • Check that the website is live and loading correctly
  • Update the "We're Buying Today" section on the homepage if needed
