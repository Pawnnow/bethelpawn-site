🔑 Pawning

How does pawning work at Bethel Pawn? +
Bring your item in during business hours with a valid government-issued ID. We'll appraise it on the spot at no charge, make you a cash offer, and if you accept you walk out with cash immediately. You then have a set period (typically 30–90 days) to pay back the loan amount plus a fee to reclaim your item. If you choose not to redeem it, there's no penalty — we simply keep the item.
What do I need to bring to pawn an item? +
You need your item and a valid, government-issued photo ID (driver's license, state ID, or passport). For firearms, Ohio law requires additional documentation — see our Firearms section below.
How long do I have to redeem my item? +
Redemption periods are typically 30–90 days depending on the item and loan amount. We'll give you clear terms in writing before you accept any loan. You can also extend or renew your pawn — just come in before your redemption date.
What happens if I don't redeem my item? +
Nothing happens to you — there's no penalty, no credit impact, no collections. You simply forfeit the item. The loan is satisfied and we keep the item to sell in our inventory.
What are your pawn fees and interest rates? +
Fees and rates vary by loan amount and item type and are regulated by Ohio law. We'll always show you the full terms — total to repay, due date, and fee — before you commit to anything. Call us at (513) 427-4705 for current rate information.

💰 Selling

What items do you buy? +
We buy a wide range of items including firearms, gold and scrap gold, jewelry and diamonds, coins and bullion, power tools (Milwaukee, DeWalt, Snap-On), electronics, musical instruments, sporting goods, luxury items, and video games. If it has value, bring it in — we'll take a look.
How do you determine what you'll pay? +
We research current market values, condition, demand, and resale potential. For gold and silver we use live spot prices. For tools, electronics, and other items we check current retail and resale markets. We aim to give you the best offer we can while keeping it fair for both sides.
Do items need to be in perfect condition? +
No — condition affects the offer but doesn't disqualify an item. We buy scrap gold regardless of condition, and we buy used tools, electronics, and other items even if they show wear. Just bring it in and let us take a look.
How quickly will I get paid? +
Same day, same visit. If you accept our offer you walk out with cash in hand before you leave the store.

🛒 Buying

Can I buy items at Bethel Pawn? +
Yes — we have a rotating inventory of firearms, tools, electronics, jewelry, instruments, and more. Stop in to see what's currently available. Inventory changes frequently so it's worth checking back regularly.
Do you sell online? +
Yes, some items are listed on eBay. Check our eBay store for current listings. For firearms, all sales must be completed in-store with proper ID and background check.

🔫 Firearms & FFL

Is Bethel Pawn FFL licensed? +
Yes. Our Federal Firearms License number is FFL #4-31-0339. We are a fully licensed pawnbroker and firearms dealer in Clermont County, Ohio. All firearm transactions are fully compliant with Ohio and federal law.
Can I pawn or sell a firearm? +
Yes. We buy and pawn handguns, rifles, shotguns, ammo, and accessories. You'll need a valid government-issued photo ID. All transactions comply with Ohio and federal firearm laws including required background checks where applicable.
Do you do FFL transfers? +
Yes. If you purchase a firearm online or from an out-of-state dealer, we can receive it as your local FFL transfer dealer. Contact us at (513) 427-4705 before having the firearm shipped to confirm our current transfer fee and process.
What firearms do you buy and sell? +
We deal in handguns, rifles, shotguns, and related accessories including ammo, optics, cleaning kits, and cases. We buy most major brands in good working condition. Bring it in for a free appraisal.

✨ Gold & Jewelry

How much do you pay for gold? +
We pay based on the current live spot price of gold, adjusted for karat (10k, 14k, 18k, 24k). We test all gold on-site using professional testing equipment and give you a transparent, itemized offer. Condition doesn't matter — we buy broken, scrap, and unwanted gold jewelry.
Do you buy silver and coins? +
Yes — we are a coins and bullion specialist. We buy silver rounds, Morgan dollars, Peace dollars, gold coins, bullion bars, and currency collections. We use live spot pricing for all precious metals.
Do you buy diamond jewelry? +
Yes. We buy diamond rings, necklaces, bracelets, and other fine jewelry. Our jewelry manager Camryn will assess the piece and give you a fair offer based on metal content, stone quality, and current market value.
