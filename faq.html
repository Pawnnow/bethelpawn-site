Firearms at Bethel Pawn. Firearms are our specialty. Bethel Pawn is a federally licensed firearms dealer (FFL) and we handle all transactions legally and professionally. Whether you're buying, selling, pawning, or transferring a firearm, here's what you need to know.

Yes. Bethel Pawn is a federally licensed firearms dealer (FFL), which means we are fully authorized to buy, sell, and transfer firearms under federal and Ohio law.

To purchase a firearm from us, you will need a valid government-issued photo ID and must pass an NICS background check (the same check run at any gun store). We handle the background check paperwork on-site — it typically takes just a few minutes.

Yes. We buy and pawn firearms regularly — it is one of our most active categories. To sell or pawn a firearm you must:

• Be the legal owner of the firearm
• Present a valid, government-issued photo ID
• Be 18 or older for long guns, 21 or older for handguns
• Ensure the firearm is not reported stolen

We handle all required paperwork and ensure every transaction is fully compliant with federal ATF regulations and Ohio law. Bring the firearm unloaded when you come in.

Yes. If you purchase a firearm online or from a private seller in another state, federal law requires the gun to be shipped to a local FFL dealer for transfer to you. Bethel Pawn can serve as your receiving FFL.

Here's the process: you give the seller or online retailer our FFL information, they ship the firearm to us, you come in with your ID, complete the background check paperwork, and take possession of your firearm.

Yes. Federal law requires a background check (NICS — National Instant Criminal Background Check System) for all firearm purchases from a licensed dealer, including pawn shops. This applies to every firearm purchase, including used and pawned firearms.
