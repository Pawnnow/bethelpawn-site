Know Before You Go

Ohio Gun Laws & What to Bring

Buying or selling a gun in Ohio is straightforward — here's what you need to know before you come to Bethel Pawn.

  • 🪪

    Valid Photo ID Required

    Ohio driver's license, state ID, or U.S. passport required for all firearm transactions. Must match your current address.

  • ✅

    NICS Background Check

    Federal law requires a background check for all gun purchases from a licensed dealer. We run it on-site — results typically return in minutes.

  • 📅

    Age Requirements

    Must be 21 or older to purchase a handgun. Must be 18 or older to purchase a long gun (rifle or shotgun). Same rules apply for pawning or selling.

  • 🚗

    Bring It Unloaded

    When bringing a firearm in to sell or pawn, it must be unloaded. Transport it in accordance with Ohio law — in a case and not accessible from the passenger compartment.
