Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about pawning, buying, selling, and firearms at Bethel Pawn.

How does pawning work at Bethel Pawn? +
Bring in your item during business hours. We appraise it on the spot at no charge, make you a cash offer, and if you accept you get cash immediately. You have 30–90 days to pay back the loan plus a fee and reclaim your item — or you can let it go with no penalty.
How much do you pay for gold & jewelry? +
We pay based on live spot price for gold and silver. We test all metals on-site and give you a transparent offer. Condition doesn't matter — we buy scrap gold too.
Can I pawn or sell a firearm? +
Yes. Bethel Pawn is a fully licensed FFL dealer. We buy and pawn handguns, rifles, shotguns, and accessories. You'll need a valid government-issued ID. All transactions are fully compliant with Ohio and federal law.
Do you do FFL transfers? +
Yes, we accept FFL transfers for firearms purchased online or from out-of-state dealers. Contact us in advance to arrange the transfer and confirm our current fee.
Is Bethel Pawn FFL licensed? +
Yes. Our Federal Firearms License number is FFL #4-31-0339. We are a fully licensed pawnbroker and firearms dealer in Clermont County, Ohio.
Do you need an appointment? +
No appointment needed — ever. Walk in Tuesday through Friday 10am–5pm or Saturday 10am–2pm and we'll take care of you on the spot.
