You bring in an item, we appraise it and make you a cash offer. Leave the item with us as collateral and walk out with cash. Unlike a loan, a pawn creates no legal debt — if you choose not to redeem your item, you simply walk away. You owe nothing, there are no collections, and your credit is never affected. No credit check required.
