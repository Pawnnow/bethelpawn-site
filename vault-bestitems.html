Best Items to Pawn

Expert Guide from Bethel Pawn · Bethel, Ohio

What Makes an Item Good to Pawn?

The best items to pawn are ones with high resale demand, stable market value, and easy verification. Items that hold their value well get higher loan offers because we have confidence we can recover our money if the item is not redeemed. Here are the categories that consistently get the strongest offers at Bethel Pawn.

1. Gold & Precious Metals

Gold, silver, and platinum are always at the top of the list. The value is directly tied to the live spot price — transparent, verifiable, and consistent. We test on-site and pay based on actual karat and weight. Condition does not matter for scrap gold.

  • Gold jewelry (10k, 14k, 18k, 24k)
  • Silver coins and rounds
  • Bullion bars and coins
  • Platinum jewelry

2. Firearms

Quality firearms in good working condition consistently get strong offers. We are a licensed FFL dealer and know the current market well. Popular brands and common calibers get the best offers.

  • Handguns (Glock, Smith & Wesson, Sig Sauer, Ruger)
  • Rifles (AR-15 pattern, bolt action hunting rifles)
  • Shotguns (pump and semi-auto)

Firearms Require ID

All firearm transactions require a valid government-issued photo ID. We are FFL licensed #4-31-0339 and fully compliant with all Ohio and federal laws.

3. Quality Power Tools

Professional-grade tools from top brands hold their value well and are always in demand. Milwaukee, DeWalt, and Snap-On consistently get the strongest offers. Bring chargers and batteries — they increase your offer significantly.

  • Milwaukee M18 and M12 systems
  • DeWalt 20V MAX tools
  • Snap-On hand tools and tool sets
  • Makita cordless systems

4. Electronics

Newer devices in good working condition get solid offers. The key is condition — a cracked screen or non-working device will significantly reduce the offer. Apple products tend to hold value better than other brands.

  • iPhones and iPads (recent models)
  • Laptops (MacBooks and high-end Windows laptops)
  • Gaming consoles (PS5, Xbox Series X)

5. Jewelry & Diamonds

Fine jewelry, diamond rings, and luxury watches all get strong offers. Our jewelry manager Camryn specializes in this category and will give you a transparent, fair appraisal.

Tips for Any Item

  • Bring original packaging and accessories
  • Clean the item before bringing it in
  • Know the brand and model number if possible
  • Working condition always gets a better offer than non-working
