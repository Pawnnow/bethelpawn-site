Know Before You Come In

What Affects Your Tool Offer

  • 🏷️

    Brand Matters Most

    Milwaukee and Snap-On command significantly better prices than off-brand tools of similar function. Brand is the single biggest factor in what we pay.

  • ✅

    Working Condition

    We test tools before buying. Working tools get full offers. Tools that don't run, have broken switches, or smoke when started are much harder to sell and will be offered accordingly or declined.

  • 🔋

    Batteries & Chargers Included

    Cordless tools with their original batteries and chargers are worth significantly more than bare tools. An M18 drill with two batteries is a completely different sale than a bare tool.

  • 📦

    Original Case or Bag

    Original hard cases, Packout cases, or tool bags add to the offer — not by a huge amount, but it's worth bringing them.

  • 📊

    Current Resale Market

    We check what that specific model is actually selling for right now. Tool prices fluctuate — some older Milwaukee models are very active, others have been replaced and sit.

💡 Tips to Get the Best Offer on Your Tools

Test everything before you come in. Run each tool, make sure it powers on, and know if anything feels off. We test on-site but knowing your tools work before you come builds confidence in the offer.

Bring the batteries. Cordless tools without batteries get offers based on bare-tool market value — which is much lower. If you have the batteries, bring them even if they're old.

Bring the whole set if you have it. A 5-piece Milwaukee M18 combo kit is worth far more as a complete unit than the sum of 5 individual tools. Don't break up a set unless you have a reason to.

Know the model numbers. Milwaukee M18 Fuel vs. M18 standard makes a real difference in offer. Look at the label on the tool for the model number before you come in.

Call first for a quick estimate: (513) 427-4705 — tell us the brand and model and we'll give you a ballpark right away.

  • ✗ Very old tools with no current market demand
  • ✗ Incomplete sets where key components are missing
