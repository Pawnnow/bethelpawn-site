Our Story

Built on Honest Dealing

Bethel Pawn opened its doors in 2013 with a simple idea: treat every customer the way you'd want to be treated. No lowball offers, no confusing terms, no pressure. Just fair appraisals, straight talk, and cash in hand the same day.

LaMont and Teresa Baudendistel founded the shop in the heart of Bethel, Ohio — a small town in Clermont County where your reputation is everything. They built Bethel Pawn one customer at a time, and word spread the way it does in small communities: honestly and fast.

Over the years, Bethel Pawn became the go-to shop for the area's hunters, tradesmen, collectors, and everyday people who needed fast cash without the runaround. Firearms became the specialty — LaMont's deep knowledge of guns and fair pricing earned a loyal following across Clermont County and beyond.

"We built this business on the idea that people deserve to be treated fairly — whether they're pawning a gun or buying one. That hasn't changed in twelve years and it never will." — LaMont Baudendistel, Founder

Now, Brayden Hall — LaMont and Teresa's grandson — has stepped in as General Manager, carrying the same values into the next chapter of Bethel Pawn's story. Same family, same standards, same commitment to the community that built this business.

Meet the Team

When you walk into Bethel Pawn, you're not dealing with a corporate chain. You're dealing with real people who know their stuff — and who actually live here. Every member of our team brings something different to the table, and together they're the reason customers keep coming back.

LaMont Baudendistel

Founder & Owner
Firearms Expert

LaMont started Bethel Pawn in 2013 with one guiding principle: give people a straight answer. His background in firearms runs deep — he can identify, appraise, and speak to just about any gun that comes through the door. "The Old Man and The Gun" didn't get that nickname by accident. More than a decade in, he still shows up every day with the same no-nonsense approach that built this business from scratch.

Teresa Baudendistel

Co-Owner
Operations & HR

Teresa has been the backbone of Bethel Pawn since day one. While LaMont works the floor, Teresa keeps everything else running — from personnel and compliance to making sure every customer interaction meets the standard she and LaMont set when they opened. If the shop runs like a well-oiled machine, it's because of her. She's the reason new employees learn the right way to do things from the start.

Brayden Hall

Store Manager
Operations & Inventory

Brayden grew up around this business and knows it inside and out. As Store Manager, he's the one keeping daily operations on track — managing inventory, handling compliance paperwork, and making sure the shop runs the way LaMont and Teresa intended. He's the first call when something needs to get done, and usually the one who already did it. The next generation of Bethel Pawn is in good hands.

Mike Boehme

Assistant Manager
Sales Floor & Customer Service

Mike is the guy you're most likely to meet when you walk through the door. As Assistant Manager, he keeps the sales floor sharp — new items up, everything organized, and customers taken care of. He's Brayden's right hand and the kind of person who just gets things handled. Whether you're browsing, selling, or just have a question, Mike's probably already headed your way.

Camryn Hall

Jewelry Manager
Jewelry & Appraisal

Camryn handles every piece of jewelry that comes through Bethel Pawn — cleaning, pricing, and making sure it's displayed the way it deserves to be. She's also currently enrolled in jewelry school, studying repair and restoration to bring even more expertise to the case. If you're bringing in gold, silver, or stones, Camryn is the one who gives it a proper look.

Our History

The Bethel Pawn Story

2013

Bethel Pawn Opens Its Doors

LaMont and Teresa Baudendistel open Bethel Pawn at 119 West Plane St in Bethel, Ohio. The focus from day one: firearms expertise, fair pricing, and honest service for the Clermont County community.

2015

FFL License Established

Bethel Pawn becomes a federally licensed firearms dealer (FFL), allowing full buy/sell/pawn and transfer services for firearms. This cements firearms as the shop's primary specialty.

2018

A Trusted Name in Clermont County

Five years in, Bethel Pawn has built a loyal following across Bethel, Batavia, Owensville, and surrounding communities. Hundreds of five-star reviews reflect what the community has known since 2013.

2024

The Next Generation Steps In

Grandson Brayden Hall joins as General Manager, bringing fresh energy to a business built on timeless values. The Baudendistel family legacy continues into its second decade.

Now

Still Serving Bethel, Ohio Every Day

Bethel Pawn is open Tuesday through Friday 10am–5pm and Saturday 10am–2pm at 119 West Plane St, Bethel, OH. Walk-ins always welcome — no appointment needed.
